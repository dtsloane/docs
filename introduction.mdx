## Getting Started
### Onboarding 
- After signing up, you should get an invite to Cambrean via `email`. Now it's time to login and go through the onboarding process. Struggling to login? <a href="mailto:hello@cambrean.com">Contact us.</a>
- You can login through your Google Account, or we can send a `SSO` link to your email address
- Fill out the information fields while you're going through the onboarding process. Now it's time to centralize your data!
