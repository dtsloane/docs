Privacy is at the core of what we're building here at Cambrean. We believe your health data should be in your control, always. We are committed to protecting your sensive health data, which is why we do the following : 

    1. Once you disconnect your devices, all your data is removed from our database 
    2. Once you delete your account, all of your data is removed from our database 

<Tip>
We never share your data with anyone. You're in full control of your information. 
</Tip>

## Storing your data 
### Encryption
We use Evervault to encrypt all data at REST and in transit. Furthermore, we encrypt the data prior to storing it it in our database.

>This means your data is always anonymous in our database
